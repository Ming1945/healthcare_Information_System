About Us

As a key player in the healthcare system, we take care of the medical care of the population. We ensure that there are an adequate number of outpatient doctors and psychotherapists for the nearly eight million people in Lower Saxony. They are meant to help patients prevent and alleviate diseases, as well as treat them according to the latest medical standards. To maintain this, we particularly advocate for the interests of our members – the outpatient, authorized, or employed doctors, psychological psychotherapists, as well as child and adolescent psychotherapists in Lower Saxony – to both the government and health insurance companies.


We advise our members on all matters related to setting up practices, managing them, and billing. We support you in ensuring the quality of your practice and provide extensive training opportunities.


Our core task is the so-called "guarantee mandate." It states that the KVen guarantee to health insurance companies that all contract medical services are provided properly.
